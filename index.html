A Pirate's Guide to the Galaxy

Hello, my name is Keyuna and I go to the Haringey Pirates. Every week in the lockdown I do the Literacy Pirates writing challenges, and for this project we have invented our own planets and described how they are. I hope you enjoy listening to them!

Keyuna, Young Pirate

Welcome to A Pirate's Guide to The Galaxy, a place where you can explore imaginary worlds without even leaving your chair! Each planet has been invented and described by a participant of The Literacy Pirates who were asked to write and record pieces that would help transport their listener to a far away place where they could feel calm and relaxed. Click on the video below to hear children's author Konnie Huq tell us more about the project.
